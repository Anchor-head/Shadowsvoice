Baby What?


Baby Stormer publishes polished, safe-for-work renditions of articles from The Daily Stormer, a highly censored internet publication.

Andrew Anglin, the man behind the infamous publication, is an icon of everything the Western world hates and fears: anti-semitism, mysoginy, and homophobia are recurring themes in his stories. Couple that with a provocative writing style and a foul mouth (or pen... or keyboard, rather), and you have The Daily Stormer, a publication which, to no one's surprise, has changed its domain over a dozen times to evade total censorship.

However, some of the geopolitical reporting burried in this dirt.

Yet, there is a baby in the storm, good in the bad, a white dot in the Yin.
